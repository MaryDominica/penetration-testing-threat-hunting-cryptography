# Discovery Phase

## Overview
The discovery phase focuses on information gathering to understand
the target environment within authorised boundaries.

## Purpose
- Identify potential attack surfaces
- Gather information for risk assessment
- Support informed testing decisions

## Discovery Techniques
- Passive reconnaissance
- Active reconnaissance

## Ethical Considerations
All discovery activities must follow the defined scope and rules
of engagement to avoid unauthorised access or disruption.

## Key Takeaway
The discovery phase lays the foundation for effective and responsible
security assessment.
