# Scope and Rules of Engagement

## Penetration Testing Scope
The scope delineates the areas and systems to be tested and includes:
- Scope specification
- Resource allocation
- Risk assessment
- Communication plan
- Legal and compliance considerations

## Rules of Engagement
Rules of engagement establish how the test will be conducted and include:
- Scope definition
- Testing methods
- Timing and duration
- Authorised personnel
- Communication protocols
- Legal and ethical considerations
- Incident response plan
- Post-testing procedures

## Importance
Clear scope and rules of engagement:
- Minimise potential conflicts and misunderstandings
- well-define rules promote a collaborative and constructive approach toward enhancing an organisational security posture
- protects an org, from any intended disruption or damages during the testing process. 

## Key Takeaway
Scope and rules of engagement provide structure, safety, and clarity
throughout the penetration testing process.
